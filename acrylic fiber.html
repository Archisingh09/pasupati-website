Introduction

Acrylic fiber is a synthetic fiber made from polyacrylonitrile. It is soft, lightweight, warm, and often used as a wool substitute in textiles and fabrics.

Properties

Soft & Lightweight
UV Resistant
Quick Drying
Easily Dyed

Uses of Acrylic Fiber

  • Clothing (Sweaters, Scarves, Gloves)
  • Home Furnishings (Curtains, Upholstery)
  • Outdoor Fabrics
  • Blankets & Throws

Advantages & Disadvantages

Advantages

  • Affordable alternative to wool
  • Lightweight and warm
  • Durable and colorfast

Disadvantages

  • Not breathable
  • Melts under high heat
  • Non-biodegradable
