Company Profile

About Acrylon Business

We started commercial operations in 1990, in collaboration with SNIA BPD Italy of the FIAT group. Through constant development and innovation, we've reached 45,000 MT/year in acrylic fiber capacity.

We integrate technology, automation, and process control systems to ensure highly reliable, efficient operations.

Wide Product Range

Pasupati products span Denier (0.9–15.0), Cut lengths (38–150mm), Luster (Semi-dull to Super Bright), and Dyeing (Gel, Tow). They serve Sweaters, Blankets, Shawls, and Upholstery markets.

About CPP Film Business

With 10,000 MT/year capacity, our CPP Film Division features German and Italian machinery, meeting BRCGS hygiene standards.

Our films range from 15–80 microns, supporting bread packaging, anti-fog, medical grade, and peelable applications.

We are one of the leading producers of Acrylic fiber. We started commercial operations in 1990 with technical collaboration with SNIA BPD Italy.
