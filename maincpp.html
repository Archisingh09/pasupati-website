About Us

Pasupati Acrylon Ltd. is one of the leading producers of Acrylon Fiber in India since 1990. Having established ourselves in the Acrylic marcket, we have been looking out for newer avenues and have diversifed into production of CPP Films with an installed capacity of 10,000 MT per annum.
This state of art manufacturing unit of CPP Film cast Line has been imported from Germany and Italy. vacuum metalizer & advance slitting Machine from U.K. We are producing Film of thickness from 15 micron to 150 micron such as NaturalCPP (Lamination grade)/ White opaque/ Met CPP/ Medical grade/ Bread packaging etc.

About CPP Film Business

Our CPP Film meets the requirements set out in the BRC GLOBAL STANDARD for PACKAGING AND PACKING MATERIALS ( ISSUE 5: JULY 2015) and is categorised as HIGH HYGINE Grade-A by the BRCGS.

We are committed to total customer satisfaction by identifying their specif needs translating them into quality products and providing dependable after sales support, resulting in TOTAL CUSTOMER DELIGHT. Our CPP film is widely accepted PAN INDIA and is known for excellent quality and consistency, we have also exported to countries like Nepal, Bahrain and other GCC countries.

We are one of the leading producers of Acrylic fiber. We started commercial operations in 1990 with technical collaboration with SNIA BPD Italy.
