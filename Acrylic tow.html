Acrylic Tow

High-quality synthetic fiber used in textiles and more

What is Acrylic Tow?

Acrylic Tow is a form of acrylic fiber, often used in the production of yarn, textiles, and industrial materials. It is known for its softness, warmth, and resistance to chemicals and sunlight.

Advantages of Acrylic Tow

  • Lightweight and soft
  • Resistant to UV and chemicals
  • Quick drying
  • Used in knitting, weaving, and industrial processes

Applications

Acrylic Tow is used in:

  • Textile yarns
  • Wool replacement products
  • Wigs and hair extensions
  • Blankets and carpets
