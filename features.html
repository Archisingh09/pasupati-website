Our Human Resource Policy

Human resource management in the company has assumed greater importance for bringing about sustained growth, organizational effectiveness and individual satisfaction. The company believes that the personnel policies should be dynamic and adaptive to create proper working environment.

The employees are encouraged to work and think independently in furtherance of the company’s objectives and philosophies. The company also has different training and development programmes. The role of the training is not only to update the knowledge of the employees but also to develop them to assume higher responsibilities in course of time.

Identifying Star Performers

Training for upgrading skills, knowledge and mental abilities

A scientifically designed and tested appraisal system is in place to assess the performance and potential of each employee. It helps in identifying star performers for suitable raise and growth. It also helps to ascertain the areas where employees need help and improvement to put forth their best performance.

The focus on the human resources has made Pasupati Acrylon unique among all organisations in the location and a leader in the industry in terms of quality and productivity. Due to employee oriented Human Relation Policies, majority of our employees are working for decades in the organization as Pasupati family.

We are one of the leading producers of Acrylic fiber. We started commercial operations in 1990 with technical collaboration with SNIA BPD Italy.
