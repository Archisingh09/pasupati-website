Environment & Safety Initiatives

We Care for Human and Material Safety

As a responsible corporate citizen, Pasupati Acrylon is very much alive to the need of caring for human and material safety, the concern for the environmental protection and conservation of natural resources.

In view of the nature of chemicals used in the manufacturing of Acrylic fibre, the best possible safety systems against fire, air and water pollution have been installed.

Safety and Environment Concerns

Smoke Detectors

Smoke detectors with alarm systems have been installed across all fire-prone zones, ensuring rapid response to fire emergencies.

Water & Air Treatment

We use low-pollution technology and advanced treatment for both water and air effluents to minimize environmental impact.

Growth Of Plants

Treated water is used for irrigation and horticulture, which promotes the healthy growth of plants in and around our premises.

Modern Effluent Water Treatment Plant

Spanning 90 acres, with a boundary of 10,000+ trees and 80m wide forest belt, our plant restores ecological balance and enhances factory aesthetics.

Auto Fire Prevention Systems and Alarms

Well-designed fire prevention systems are periodically tested and maintained for peak accuracy, keeping the factory safe from emergencies.

Modern & Efficient Rain Water Harvesting System

Equipped with a modern ESP & boiler, our setup ensures low emissions. We conserve water via efficient harvesting systems for sustainable use.

We are one of the leading producers of Acrylic fiber. We started commercial operations in 1990 with technical collaboration with SNIA BPD Italy.
