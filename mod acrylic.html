PASUPATI MODACRYLIC FIBER

MOD Acrylic

The generic name of a fiber that has a lower acrylonitrile (35-85%) level than normal acrylic fiber in its make-up. Pasupati Mod-acrylic fiber developed by PASUPATI ACRYLON LTD (SNIA TECHNOLOGY) that possesses the excellent flame retardancy as well as acrylic fibers natural attributes of softness and dyeability.

Application Of Mod-acrylic Fiber

1. Protective Clothing

Flame retardancy and self extinguishing ability as well as resistance against chemicals, protects Industrial workers from any injuries.

2. Hair extensions and wigs

Mod Acrylic is also used as a realistic alternative to human hair in making of hair extensions, wigs and doll hairs.

3.Battery material:

It is used in electrodes of lead –acid batteries, alkali batteries and parts of separators
