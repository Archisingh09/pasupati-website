White CPP

WOP CPP Film

This WOP CPP Film is categorically formulated to deliver excellent opacity, strength, stiffness along with outstanding heat sealing feature. Application uses of this WOP is done as lamination substrates for packs used for Food, Baby Diaper Packs, Dry Food Packs like Biscuits, Detergent and several other materials for packing.

Natural CPP

Transparent CPP Films

These transparent CPP Films are basically ultra clear Cast Film which is specially designed for offering excellent performance with great appearance and easy conversion to do flexible packaging.

Metalized CPP

Improved Properties

Plazma and Corona treatment facility of this Met CPP Film helps to increase the shelf life & barrier properties. It also provides a metallic Glossy Appearance which resembles the look of aluminium foil
